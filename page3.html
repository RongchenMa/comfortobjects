Childhood Toys

Sometimes, a small object can bring immense comfort. It might be a childhood teddy bear, an old scarf, or a little charm you never part with. In moments of chaos or loneliness, it reminds you—you are not alone. A comfort object doesn't need to be perfect; it simply belongs to you, quietly keeping you company. It is the calm of night, the warmth on rainy days, a small buffer when you're on the edge of breaking. A comfort object doesn't solve problems; it provides a temporary space to hold your emotions. It can be a habitual gesture, a sound, a shape, a familiar touch. Everyone's comfort object is different, and it's precisely this uniqueness that makes it real.
