Pillows...

I sometimes wonder if everyone has their own "comfort object". For me, it's this pillow.




That's right. It's just an object, but when you hug it, it's as if the whole world goes quiet.






Sometimes I feel like it understands me. If I'm not sleeping well, feeling sad or confused, I find that the moment I touch it, I start to feel better.





This sounds a bit magical, but I like this trick. Even when everything else changes, it stays the same.


Yes, I understand what you mean. Mine has got old and a bit yellow, but I don't want to replace it.




Perhaps that's just a small act of kindness we show ourselves. It reminds me of the toy we used to hold onto when we couldn't sleep as children, but now, as adults, it has taken on a different form.






That's the sense of something being familiar. It remembers your warmth, your tears, and all those nights when you didn't say anything.





Maybe everyone needs something like this. It doesn't speak, it doesn't offer answers—it just quietly exists, letting you feel... you're still wrapped in some kind of tenderness.
