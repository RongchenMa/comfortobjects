[Comfort Object]

This is a website dedicated to my collection of comfort objects.
It documents the small items that provide me with a sense of security and emotional solace in daily life—perhaps insignificant, yet becoming pillars of support in specific moments.
They are not merely objects on display, but vessels of emotion, memory, and attachment, inviting reflection on the meaning of “comfort” in our lives.
